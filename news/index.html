Fazeshift Raises $17M Series A for AI Agents That Run Accounts Receivable

Fazeshift, which deploys AI agents to automate accounts receivable — invoicing, follow-ups, reconciliation, and cash application — raised a $17 million Series A led by F-Prime Capital, with participation from Google's Gradient Ventures and Y Combinator. Investors are betting on AI-powered finance operations starting with AR, the transactional-finance segment where AI results have been strongest.

Why it mattersInvoice-to-cash is where finance AI demonstrably pays back today — capital flowing to AR-specific agents confirms transactional finance is the beachhead before AI moves up-stack to FP&A.

Try itMeasure your DSO and manual cash-application hours; if either is material, AR agents are currently the highest-probability finance-AI ROI to pilot.
