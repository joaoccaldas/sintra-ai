**2. KEY DAX MEASURES:**
```dax
Gross Margin % =
DIVIDE([Gross Profit], [Total Revenue], BLANK())

Budget Variance % =
DIVIDE([Total Revenue] - SUM('Budget'[Revenue]), SUM('Budget'[Revenue]))

EBITDA =
[Gross Profit] - [Total OPEX] + [D&A]
```

"Build a P&L summary page with: a KPI row (Revenue, Gross Margin %, EBITDA, Headcount), a waterfall chart showing Revenue → Gross Profit → EBITDA bridge, and a small multiples line chart of Revenue by Department vs prior year. Add a Month slicer."
