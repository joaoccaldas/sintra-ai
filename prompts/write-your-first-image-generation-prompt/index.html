The prompt

I want to create an image of [what you want to see]. Help me write a detailed Midjourney or DALL-E prompt that will produce a great result. Include: subject, style, lighting, mood, composition, and any negative prompts to avoid. Give me 3 variations at different style directions.

Sample output

**Variation 1 — Cinematic Photo** `/imagine a lone lighthouse on rocky cliffs at golden hour, stormy ocean in background, dramatic god rays through clouds, shot on Hasselblad, f/2.8, cinematic color grade, 8k --ar 16:9 --v 6` *Negative:* cartoonish, flat, oversaturated, text **Variation 2 — Oil Painting** `/imagine lighthouse on sea cliffs, oil painting in the style of J.M.W. Turner, impressionistic brushwork, warm ochre and deep navy palette, museum quality, impasto texture --ar 4:3 --stylize 800` *Negative:* photorealistic, digital, modern **Variation 3 — Minimal Illustration** `/imagine minimalist vector illustration of lighthouse, flat design, 3-color palette of cream/navy/coral, geometric shapes, editorial style, negative space composition --ar 1:1 --style raw` *Negative:* complex, detailed, photographic
