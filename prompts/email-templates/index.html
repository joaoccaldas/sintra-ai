Email Templates

Five reusable templates — initial outreach, follow-up, meeting request, thank-you, status update — short and actionable.

⬡
Recommended modelClaude Haiku 4.5

Fast, fluent copy generation for everyday tasks

What you need to fill in

[situation][professional/friendly/urgent]

Tools used

ChatGPT

The prompt

"I need email templates for [situation]. Please create: 1. Initial outreach 2. Follow-up after no response 3. Meeting request 4. Thank you after meeting 5. Status update Tone: [professional/friendly/urgent] Keep them short and actionable."
Open in Claude

Sample output

**Initial outreach** > Subject: [their company] × [your company] — quick idea > Hi [name], saw [specific signal]. We help [people like them] do [specific outcome]. Worth a 15-min chat next week? **Follow-up (after no reply)** > Subject: bumping this up > Hi [name], jumping back here. If now's a bad week, just let me know and I'll try again in [month]. **Meeting request** > Subject: 20 min, [topic]? > Goal: decide [X]. Tue 10am, Thu 2pm — what works? **Thank-you** > Thanks for the time. Three things I owe you: [...]. Talk [date]. **Status update** > [Project] · [colour] · [1-line headline]. Wins: [...]. Risks: [...]. Asks: [...].
