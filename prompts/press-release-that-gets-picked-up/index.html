Press Release That Gets Picked Up

A publication-ready press release in AP style with all standard elements.

Recommended modelClaude Sonnet 4.6

What you need to fill in

[announcement][company name and description][release date][spokesperson quote or ask to draft][data or market context]

Tools used

ClaudeChatGPT

The prompt

Write a press release for [announcement]. Announcement: [what you're announcing] Company: [company name, one-line description] Date: [release date] Quote from spokesperson: [give me a real quote or I'll draft one for you] Relevant context or data: [any numbers, partnerships, or market context that makes it newsworthy] Target journalists/outlets: [tech press? business press? vertical media?] Format: AP style, inverted pyramid, under 500 words. Include: dateline, headline, subheadline, boilerplate, contact info placeholder.

Sample output

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE **Volta Energy Raises $40M Series B to Scale AI-Powered Grid Management Across Latin America** *Funding will accelerate deployment in Brazil and Colombia as demand for smart grid infrastructure surges* SÃO PAULO, May 24, 2026 — Volta Energy, the AI platform helping utilities reduce grid losses by up to 35%, today announced a $40 million Series B led by Redpoint eventures, with participation from IFC and Valor Capital Group. The funding arrives as Brazil's National Electric Energy Agency (ANEEL) mandates smart metering for all utilities serving over 100,000 customers by 2028. 'Every percentage point of grid loss recovered goes directly back to lower consumer bills,' said Maria Souza, Volta's CEO. 'This funding lets us scale what we've proven in São Paulo across the entire country.' ### **About Volta Energy:** [Company boilerplate] **Media Contact:** [Name, email, phone]
