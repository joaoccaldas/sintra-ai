The prompt

I have a [game genre] with this economy: [describe currency, sources, and sinks]. Players are reporting that [specific problem: inflation / deflation / pay-to-win feeling / etc]. Diagnose the root cause, propose 3 fixes with trade-offs, and give me a spreadsheet schema I can use to model the economy before implementing changes.
Open in Claude

Sample output

**Diagnosis:** Soft currency inflation Your daily quest rewards (500 coins) outpace the primary sink (upgrades average 800 coins but only needed every 3 days). Players accumulate ~1,500 coins/day with nowhere to spend them, making purchases feel meaningless. **Fix 1 — Add a time-limited sink** Flash merchant appears daily with exclusive cosmetics for 2,000 coins. *Trade-off:* Requires art/content overhead. **Fix 2 — Reduce quest rewards by 30%** Simple lever, ships fast. *Trade-off:* Risk of player backlash; needs communication. **Fix 3 — Add a prestige/conversion system** 10,000 coins → 1 premium token. Creates an aspirational sink. *Trade-off:* Competitive pressure with IAP. **Spreadsheet schema:** | Day | Coins earned | Coins spent | Net | Running total | Active sinks |
