The prompt

Create a focused, time-boxed meeting agenda from the following information. Meeting goal: [one sentence — what must be decided, aligned, or resolved by the end?] Meeting type: [e.g., decision meeting, brainstorm, status update, retrospective, kickoff, 1:1] Attendees and roles: [list names/roles — who is the decision-maker?] Duration: [30 min / 45 min / 60 min / 90 min] Context/background: [brief context attendees need, or paste any pre-read material] Known tensions or disagreements: [anything contentious that needs careful facilitation?] Produce: 1. A pre-meeting checklist (what to send to attendees 24 hours before) 2. A time-boxed agenda with exact minutes per section and the meeting owner's role in each 3. Opening framing — the 2-sentence 'why we're here and what success looks like' statement 4. Three decision points or discussion questions, ordered from most to least critical 5. A parking lot protocol — how to handle topics that arise but are out of scope 6. A closing ritual — how to end with clear owners, actions, and deadlines 7. A post-meeting follow-up template (summary format with decisions and action items table)
