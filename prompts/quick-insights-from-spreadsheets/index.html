Sample output

**Top 3 trends** 1. Revenue grew 14% YoY, accelerating in Q3 (+22% vs. +9% Q1) 2. EMEA outpaced APAC for the first time in 18 months 3. Promo months (Mar, Sep) drove 31% of annual revenue **Charts to build** - Line: monthly revenue vs. prior year (12 + 12 points) - Stacked bar: revenue by region per quarter - Scatter: promo spend vs. incremental revenue **Unusual pattern:** August dip of −7% breaks the seasonal pattern. Worth asking ops. **Investigate** - Is the August dip a stocking issue or genuine demand? - Does the EMEA acceleration hold without the FX tailwind?
