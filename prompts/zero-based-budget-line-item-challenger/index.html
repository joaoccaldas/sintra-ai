Zero-Based Budget Line-Item Challenger

A ZBB decision table with eliminate / halve / approve recommendations for every line above threshold, with benchmarks and challenge rationale — typically identifies 15–25% addressable savings.

⬡
Recommended modelGPT-5.5 Instant

Excellent tabular logic and financial model structuring

What you need to fill in

[department][fiscal year][paste cost centre lines — account, prior year actual, current year request, variance %][threshold, e.g., $50K][growth stage, cost reduction target, strategic priorities]

Tools used

ChatGPTClaudeExcel

The prompt

"Act as a Zero-Based Budgeting (ZBB) facilitator. I'm reviewing [department]'s budget for [fiscal year]. Budget data: [paste cost centre lines — account, prior year actual, current year request, variance %] For each line item above [threshold, e.g., $50K]: 1. Ask the three ZBB challenge questions: Is this necessary? Is this the right amount? Is this the right time? 2. Benchmark against industry norms where data exists 3. Propose three challenge scenarios: Zero (eliminate), 50% (halve), 100% (approve as-is) 4. Flag items where the prior year actual was unspent by >20% 5. Output a decision table with recommended stance and rationale Business context: [growth stage, cost reduction target, strategic priorities]"
Open in ChatGPT

Sample output

| Account | PY Actual | Request | ZBB Stance | Rationale | |---------|-----------|---------|------------|----------| | External consulting | $420K | $490K | 50% — $245K | 40% unspent in PY; consolidate to 2 firms | | Software licences | $180K | $210K | Approve | Per-seat growth aligned to headcount plan | | Travel & entertainment | $95K | $110K | Zero — video-first policy | Benchmark: $800/head vs. $1,400 requested | | Training | $60K | $85K | 50% — $43K | Internal AI upskilling covers 60% of need | **Addressable savings identified: $312K (22% of reviewed spend)**
