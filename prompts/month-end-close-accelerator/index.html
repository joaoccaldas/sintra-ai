The prompt

"You are a financial controller. Help me compress our month-end close from [current days] to [target days]. Current close process: - Day 1: [describe activities] - Days 2-3: [describe] - Days 4-5: [describe] - Biggest bottleneck: [describe] - ERP / system: [e.g., SAP, NetSuite, Oracle] - Team size: [number] Please: 1. Map our close process against the Fast Close benchmark (top quartile: ≤3 days) 2. Identify the top 3 bottlenecks and root causes 3. Suggest specific automation or process changes for each bottleneck 4. Draft a 30-60-90 day implementation roadmap 5. Write the journal entry review checklist for accruals, prepayments, and intercompany eliminations"

Sample output

**Bottleneck #1: Intercompany reconciliation (Day 3–4)** Root cause: 12 entities post in different time zones; no automated matching Fix: Implement continuous matching in NetSuite (native IC module) + hard cutoff at D+1 18:00 UTC Estimated saving: 0.8 days **Journal Entry Review Checklist** - [ ] Accrued expenses: all POs >$10K with no invoice matched? - [ ] Prepayments: confirm amortisation schedule updated for any new contracts - [ ] Payroll accrual: H2 headcount change reflected? - [ ] Intercompany: all IC invoices matched and eliminated? - [ ] Revenue: deferred revenue roll-forward agrees to contract schedule?
