The prompt

Create a complete project kickoff brief from the following description. Project name: [project name] Project description: [what this project will produce and why it matters] Sponsor/stakeholder: [who commissioned this and what they care about most] Team members: [names and roles] Deadline: [target completion date or launch date] Budget: [budget or 'not yet defined'] Known dependencies: [systems, teams, vendors, or approvals needed] Known risks: [anything that could derail this — or 'none identified yet'] Definition of done: [how will you know the project is complete and successful?] Produce a complete kickoff brief with: 1. Problem statement (2 sentences: the 'before' state and why it's a problem) 2. Success criteria — 3–5 measurable outcomes tied to the definition of done 3. Scope statement — in scope (bullet list) and explicitly out of scope (bullet list) 4. Milestone plan — 4–6 milestones with target dates and owners 5. RACI summary — who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed for the top 5 decisions 6. Risk register — top 5 risks with likelihood (H/M/L), impact (H/M/L), and mitigation action 7. Communication plan — cadence, channel, and audience for status updates 8. First-week actions — a numbered checklist of the first 5 things to do to start strong
