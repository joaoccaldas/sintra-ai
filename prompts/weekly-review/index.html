The prompt

Guide me through a structured weekly review and next-week planning session. This week's highlights (wins, completions): [list anything you finished or that went well] This week's struggles (what didn't get done, what felt hard): [list incomplete items and friction points] Top priorities for next week: [list up to 5 things that must happen next week] Upcoming deadlines or events: [meetings, launches, due dates in the next 7–14 days] Energy level this week: [low / medium / high] One thing I want to do differently next week: [any habit, routine, or approach change] Based on my input, produce: 1. A brief 'Week in Review' narrative (3–5 sentences) that frames the week's story fairly 2. Three learnings or insights from this week (including one from the struggles) 3. A prioritized task list for next week, formatted as Monday–Friday blocks with realistic time estimates 4. One thing to drop, delay, or delegate from next week's list to protect deep work time 5. A 'this week's win' affirmation to start Monday with momentum 6. One question to sit with over the weekend that will make next week more intentional
