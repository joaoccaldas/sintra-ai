Product Launch Announcement

A launch announcement in your chosen format that leads with benefits, not features.

⬡
Recommended modelClaude Sonnet 4.6

Superior tone control and long-form writing quality

What you need to fill in

[product/feature being launched][launch date][top 3 user benefits][proof or credibility][CTA and channel]

Tools used

ClaudeChatGPT

The prompt

Write a product launch announcement for [channel: press release / email to users / social post thread]. Product/feature: [what you're launching] Launch date: [date or 'today'] Top 3 benefits for users: [not features — what users can do now that they couldn't before] Proof or credibility: [beta user results, awards, partnerships] CTA: [what you want them to do: try, sign up, read more] Format: [choose channel from above] Tone: [excited-but-grounded / formal / conversational]
Open in Claude

Sample output

**Email to users — AI scheduling assistant launch** Subject: You asked for fewer calendar conflicts. Here's your answer. Starting today, your calendar syncs automatically. Every time you mark a meeting as 'important', your assistant learns your patterns and protects the time around it — so deep work blocks stop getting eaten by ad hoc requests. In beta, 1,200 users recovered an average of 6 hours per week. **What's new:** ✓ Smart buffer time — 15-minute recovery blocks auto-added after back-to-backs ✓ Focus mode detection — your status updates automatically ✓ Calendar health score — see your week before it happens [Turn it on in Settings → Calendar Sync]
