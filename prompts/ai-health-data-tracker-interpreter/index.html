The prompt

I will paste the results from my recent [blood work / health metrics]. Interpret each value, flag anything outside the optimal range (not just the lab reference range), explain what each flagged value might indicate, suggest 3 lifestyle or dietary adjustments I could discuss with my doctor, and format the output as a clear summary table followed by the action points. Do not diagnose — focus on pattern recognition and evidence-based context.
