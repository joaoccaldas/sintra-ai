The prompt

Write a compelling, bias-reduced job description for the following role. Role title: [exact job title] Team/department: [where this person sits] Level: [e.g., Senior, Staff, Lead, Principal] Top 3 things this person will own: [the most important responsibilities] Must-have qualifications: [non-negotiable skills or experience] Nice-to-have qualifications: [preferred but not required] Compensation range: [if you can share — improves applicant quality] Remote/hybrid/onsite: [work model and location] Company culture in 3 words: [e.g., 'fast, collaborative, customer-obsessed'] Write the JD with these sections: 1. Role summary (3–4 sentences) — lead with impact, not org chart position 2. What you'll do — 5–7 bulleted responsibilities starting with strong action verbs 3. What we're looking for — separate must-haves from nice-to-haves explicitly 4. What we offer — benefits, growth, culture (be specific, not generic) 5. About us — 2-sentence company description Apply inclusive language: remove gendered pronouns, avoid 'ninja/rockstar/guru', prefer 'you will' over 'must have X years'. Flag any requirement that may screen out qualified candidates unnecessarily.
