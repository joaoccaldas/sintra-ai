Jun 2026Notable

Alibaba Launches Qwen3.7-Plus — Multimodal GUI Agent Unifying Vision and Language

Alibaba released Qwen3.7-Plus, a multimodal agent model that unifies GUI and CLI operation across visual and text tasks in a single set of weights. On ScreenSpot Pro it scores 79.0% and Terminal-Bench 70.3%, leading the open-API GUI agent field. Available via Alibaba Cloud at $0.40/M input tokens with native vision for images and video up to 5 minutes.

May 2026Notable

Google Gemini Powers Smart Home Camera Event Automations in Google Home

Google began rolling out Gemini-powered intelligence to Google Home, enabling camera activity to trigger automated smart home actions. Users can set contextual rules — 'when a camera detects a package delivery, turn on porch lights and send a notification' — using natural-language instructions rather than manual rule configuration. The update marks the first time Gemini's multimodal vision capabilities are deployed inside consumer IoT devices, bridging cloud AI inference to residential hardware.

May 2026Notable

OpenAI Codex Ships Appshots and Goal Mode GA for Autonomous Dev Loops

OpenAI released a major Codex update on May 21 with two headline features: Appshots, a macOS hotkey (⌘⌘) that injects a screenshot plus full extracted text from any frontmost app window directly into Codex as live context; and Goal Mode reaching general availability across the Codex CLI, IDE extension, and app, letting developers define an outcome and success criteria and let Codex run autonomous multi-step loops until completion. ChatGPT Business workspaces also gained team-level plugin sharing and admin analytics for tracking code acceptance rates.

May 2026Notable

OpenAI Launches GPT-Realtime-2 With Three New Voice API Models

OpenAI released GPT-Realtime-2 alongside three new real-time audio API models optimised for live audio agents, real-time translation, and streaming transcription. The new tier adds larger context windows, adjustable reasoning effort for latency/quality trade-offs, tool transparency (the model explains what it's doing before calling a function), and stronger recovery behaviour after interruptions. The release targets voice-first product builders who need enterprise-grade multi-language support and lower-latency turn-taking.

May 2026Notable

Spotify Launches ElevenLabs-Powered AI Audiobook Narration for Self-Published Authors

At Investor Day 2026, Spotify unveiled an AI audiobook creation tool for its Spotify for Authors platform, powered by ElevenLabs voice synthesis, allowing authors to generate professional narrations without a recording studio. The tool launches in invite-only beta in June 2026, initially supporting English with expansion to 10 languages including Swedish planned for late 2026. Spotify hosts over 700,000 audiobook titles and sees this as a key path to growing its long-tail catalog without recording costs.

May 2026Major

Google Launches Gemini Omni — Any-to-Any Multimodal Generation Model

Google unveiled Gemini Omni at I/O 2026, a new model family capable of generating outputs in any modality (video, images, text) from any input, starting with video generation using realistic physics understanding. Gemini Omni Flash is immediately available to all Google AI Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers globally, as well as free to users in YouTube Shorts Remix. The model marks Google's push toward truly universal multimodal AI — what the company calls 'world understanding' — going well beyond text-and-image to native video synthesis.
