May 2026Major

Anthropic Releases Claude Opus 4.8 — 69.2% SWE-Bench Pro, 2.5× Faster

Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.8 on May 28, available on Claude API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry. Agentic coding score improved from 64.3% to 69.2% on SWE-Bench Pro, knowledge-work score jumped from 1,753 to 1,890, and the new fast mode is 2.5× faster at 3× lower cost. New capabilities include Dynamic Workflows (parallel subagent orchestration) and an effort-control toggle. Pricing remains $5/$25 per million tokens.

May 2026Major

Anthropic's Restricted Claude Mythos Surfaces in Claude Code Interface, Signaling Broader Rollout

On May 25–26, 2026, the model string claude-mythos-1-preview appeared briefly in Claude Code and Claude Security UI strings and users saw a Mythos model toggle before it disappeared, signaling Anthropic is testing an expansion of the previously restricted frontier model beyond its closed Project Glasswing program. Claude Mythos was initially launched in April 2026 with restricted access due to its ability to autonomously discover and exploit zero-day vulnerabilities across all major operating systems. Anthropic has stated it will only open Mythos more broadly once stronger misuse safeguards are in place.

May 2026Major

Pentagon Tests OpenAI and Google Models Across Five Theater Commands to Replace Anthropic

Bloomberg reported the Pentagon is evaluating AI models from OpenAI and Google across five military theater commands worldwide, seeking replacements for Anthropic's Claude after Defense Secretary Hegseth designated Anthropic a 'supply chain risk' in March 2026 following its refusal to allow Claude for autonomous weapons or mass civilian surveillance. Anthropic sued the DoD; a federal appeals court panel called the designation 'spectacular overreach' during May 2026 oral arguments. A final ruling is expected in Q3 2026.

May 2026Major

Anthropic and Gates Foundation Commit $200M to AI for Global Health, Education, and Agriculture

Anthropic and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation committed a combined $200 million in grants, Claude API credits, and technical support over four years to deploy AI in global health, education, and economic mobility, targeting the 4.6 billion people lacking access to essential health services. Priority areas include AI-accelerated vaccine and therapy development, foundational literacy tools in sub-Saharan Africa and India, and agricultural AI for the nearly 2 billion people dependent on smallholder farming. It is the largest AI-philanthropic deal of its kind.

May 2026Major

SpaceX IPO Filing Reveals Anthropic Paying $1.25B/Month for xAI's Colossus GPU Cluster — $45B Total

SpaceX's IPO prospectus disclosed that Anthropic is paying xAI $1.25 billion per month through May 2029 for exclusive access to the Colossus 1 data center in Memphis, Tennessee, covering over 300 megawatts and more than 220,000 Nvidia GPUs — totalling approximately $45 billion over the contract term. xAI retains the Colossus 2 facility for its own Grok models. Both parties hold a 90-day termination option, and capacity ramped up in May–June 2026 at an initial discounted rate.

May 2026Landmark

Anthropic Closes $30B+ Round at $900B+ Valuation — Surpasses OpenAI as Most Valuable Private Company

Bloomberg reported on May 22–23 that Anthropic is finalizing a funding round exceeding $30 billion at a pre-money valuation above $900 billion, surpassing OpenAI ($852 billion) to become the most valuable private company in the world. Sequoia, Dragoneer, Altimeter, and Greenoaks are co-leading at approximately $2 billion each, with Founders Fund and General Catalyst also participating. Anthropic reported Q2 2026 revenue of $10.9 billion — more than double Q1 — and projected annualized revenue to exceed $50 billion by end of June 2026.
