Jun 2026Major

Mistral AI Breaks Ground on €1.2B Swedish AI Datacenter with EcoDataCenter in Borlänge

Mistral AI and EcoDataCenter formally broke ground on a €1.2 billion AI datacenter facility in Borlänge, Sweden — Mistral's first AI infrastructure investment outside France. The facility will host NVIDIA Vera Rubin GPUs and is scheduled to open in 2027, making Sweden one of Europe's largest recipients of dedicated AI infrastructure investment from a European AI lab.

Jun 2026Major

NVIDIA Open-Sources Cosmos 3 Omni-Model for Physical AI and Robotics

NVIDIA released Cosmos 3, a multimodal foundation model combining vision, language, and physics simulation for robotics and autonomous systems. Released under the NVIDIA Open Model License, Cosmos 3 can generate photorealistic synthetic training environments, predict object dynamics, and control robot arms with sub-millimetre precision in simulation. It ships with integrations for Isaac Sim, ROS 2, and OpenUSD.

May 2026Major

Mistral AI Launches 'Vibe' Industrial Platform with Airbus and BMW as Launch Partners

Mistral AI unveiled Vibe, an enterprise platform for deploying on-premise AI agents in manufacturing and aerospace settings. Airbus uses Vibe to automate technical documentation review and defect classification in aircraft assembly; BMW deploys it for quality-control inspection on production lines. Vibe runs on air-gapped infrastructure using Mistral Medium 3 and supports custom fine-tuning on proprietary factory data.

May 2026Notable

Mistral AI Lands Airbus and BMW as Enterprise Clients for Sovereign Industrial AI

Mistral AI announced industrial AI deployment partnerships with Airbus and BMW in May 2026 for its Le Chat Enterprise platform, focusing on manufacturing process optimisation, engineering documentation search, and on-premises code generation. Both clients cited European data sovereignty and GDPR compliance as primary reasons for choosing Mistral over US providers. Mistral also confirmed a major groundbreaking event with Swedish EcoDataCenter for a new Nordic AI inference region.

May 2026Notable

Together AI Open-Sources OSCAR: 2-Bit KV Cache Quantization for 4x Inference Throughput

Together AI published and open-sourced OSCAR (Offline Spectral Covariance-Aware Rotation) on May 25, 2026, an INT2 KV cache quantization method integrated into SGLang's production serving stack that achieves a 5.3x memory footprint reduction and 4.1x throughput improvement over BF16 baselines. OSCAR addresses the channel-wise outlier problem that previously made 2-bit KV compression impractical by using rotation and clipping to normalize activation distributions before quantization. Sink tokens (first 64) and recent tokens (last 256) are stored in BF16, with all history tokens quantized to INT2.

May 2026Major

TanStack Supply-Chain Attack (CVE-2026-45321, CVSS 9.6) Hits OpenAI, Mistral, EU Commission

Security researchers disclosed that the TeamPCP threat group exploited CVE-2026-45321 (CVSS 9.6) to embed malicious code in the TanStack router ecosystem — a widely used JavaScript routing library. Confirmed victims include OpenAI, Mistral AI, the European Commission, and recruiting platform Mercor, highlighting growing supply-chain vulnerabilities in AI company frontend infrastructure. The incident triggered emergency patching across the JavaScript ecosystem and renewed industry-wide discussion about dependency chain security.
