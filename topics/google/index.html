Jul 2026Notable

White House in Advanced Talks With OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic on Voluntary Frontier Standards

Reports on July 3 said the White House is in advanced talks with OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic to finalize a voluntary standards framework for frontier AI model releases, covering benchmarks, testing timelines, and access rules. The framework is widely seen as the gating event for OpenAI expanding GPT-5.6 beyond its roughly 20 government-vetted partner organizations.

Jun 2026Notable

Google Ships Gemini 3.5 Live Translate for Real-Time Speech-to-Speech Translation

Google's June AI roundup introduced Gemini 3.5 Live Translate, an audio model for live speech-to-speech translation that auto-detects more than 70 languages while preserving the speaker's natural intonation, targeting multilingual calls, meetings, and travel. The month also brought Nano Banana 2 Lite as Google's fastest image model and Gemini Omni Flash reaching APIs in public preview.

Jun 2026Notable

Google Makes 'Computer Use' a Built-In Tool in Gemini 3.5 Flash

Google announced that computer use is now a built-in tool in Gemini 3.5 Flash, letting developers build agents that can interact across platforms and software interfaces directly through the model. It extends the agentic capabilities Google showcased at I/O 2026 into a generally available developer tool on its fast, lower-cost Flash tier.

Jun 2026Notable

Google DeepMind and A24 Form AI Filmmaking Research Partnership

Google DeepMind and independent studio A24 announced a partnership to explore how artificial intelligence can support filmmakers and creative professionals. The agreement brings a frontier AI laboratory into direct research collaboration with a major film studio.

Jun 2026Major

Brazilian Tupann Model Forecasts Rain Three Hours Ahead

Researchers at IMPA developed Tupann, an AI model that forecasts rainfall up to three hours ahead using satellite data rather than depending on ground radar. Google Brazil and the City of Rio supported the project, and Rio has already used the system.

Jun 2026Major

AlphaFold Scientist John Jumper Leaves DeepMind for Anthropic

Nobel Prize-winning scientist John Jumper, a co-creator of AlphaFold, announced he is leaving Google DeepMind to join Anthropic. The move adds a leading AI-for-science researcher to Anthropic amid intensifying competition for frontier talent.
