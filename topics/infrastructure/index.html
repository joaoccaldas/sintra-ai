Jun 2026Major

South Korea Unveils an $880 Billion, 10-Year AI, Chip and Robotics Plan

President Lee Jae-myung announced a national plan to invest at least $880 billion (about 1,350 trillion won) over the next decade in semiconductors, AI data centers, and robotics — his 'Three Mega Projects.' Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix are expected to put in roughly 800 trillion won (~$518 billion) to build new chip-production hubs in the country's southwest, while groups including SK, GS, and Naver back large-scale AI data-center construction with about 550 trillion won ($356 billion).

Jun 2026Major

Qualcomm Unveils 'Dragonfly' Data-Center CPU and Signs Meta as a Customer

Qualcomm rolled out a data-center CPU roadmap for the agentic-AI era, including a chip called Dragonfly, and named Meta as a major customer — Meta will use Qualcomm CPUs in its next-generation server fleet when production begins in 2028. Qualcomm framed the push around power-efficient compute for agentic workloads and projected over $15 billion in annual data-center revenue by fiscal 2029.

Jun 2026Major

OpenAI and Broadcom Unveil 'Jalapeño,' OpenAI's First Custom Inference Chip

OpenAI and Broadcom unveiled Jalapeño, OpenAI's first custom AI accelerator, a blank-slate design built specifically for LLM inference rather than adapted from general-purpose AI hardware. Co-developed from initial design to tape-out in roughly nine months, OpenAI says early results show significantly better performance-per-watt than current state-of-the-art alternatives, with initial deployment targeted by the end of 2026. Broadcom handled silicon and networking; Celestica contributed board, rack, and system integration.

Jun 2026Notable

Google Makes 'Computer Use' a Built-In Tool in Gemini 3.5 Flash

Google announced that computer use is now a built-in tool in Gemini 3.5 Flash, letting developers build agents that can interact across platforms and software interfaces directly through the model. It extends the agentic capabilities Google showcased at I/O 2026 into a generally available developer tool on its fast, lower-cost Flash tier.

Jun 2026Notable

Ericsson and Telia Launch 'Digital Arena Sweden' National 5G/6G/AI Test Center

Ericsson, Telia, RISE (and subsidiary AstaZero), Future by Lund, Lund University and KTH launched Digital Arena Sweden, a national pre-commercial 5G, 6G and AI test center announced at the Almedalen forum on Gotland. The initiative represents an investment of over SEK 300 million (about $31 million), supported by Vinnova through the Avancerad Digitalisering program, and targets industries from mining and transport to defense and medtech.

Jun 2026Notable

CoreWeave Partners With Conapto to Expand Renewable-Powered AI Cloud in Sweden

AI hyperscaler CoreWeave signed a colocation agreement with Stockholm-based Conapto spanning two campuses, with initial capacity already online at Stockholm 4 South, powered by 100% renewable energy with heat recovery into Stockholm's district heating network. The deal brings CoreWeave's European sites to eight; as of March 31, 2026 CoreWeave operated 49 data centers globally with over 1GW active and 3.5GW+ contracted power.
