TOTVS Study: 40% of Brazilian Industrial Firms Use AI but Only 2% Measure ROI

A study by TOTVS and H2R Insights found that 40% of Brazilian industrial companies report using AI in operations, yet only 2% calculate the return on investment — the lowest ratio of any sector surveyed. Overall, 93% of Brazilian companies across all sectors fail to define any success metrics for AI projects, highlighting a widespread governance gap. The findings come as Brazilian industry scales AI deployments without measurement frameworks to justify continued investment.

May 2026Notable

Exa Labs Raises $250M — AI-Native Search Becomes Critical Agentic Infrastructure

Exa Labs raised $250 million in May 2026 to scale its AI-powered semantic search infrastructure, which retrieves web content structured for LLM consumption rather than human reading. The raise validates AI-native search as a distinct infrastructure category from general web search, with demand driven by agentic pipelines that need reliable, structured information retrieval. Exa is used by leading AI companies as a backbone for research agents and RAG systems.

May 2026Notable

Ericsson and Telstra Sign 6G Research Agreement — AI-Native Standards Testing at Swedish Testbed

Ericsson and Australian carrier Telstra signed a letter of intent to jointly advance 6G research, standards evolution, and testbed trials, including reciprocal engineer exchanges between Ericsson's 6G testbed in Sweden and Telstra's Innovation Centre on the Gold Coast. The agreement formalizes joint work on 3GPP standards contributions and AI-native network capabilities, emphasizing uplink capacity improvements for agentic AI workloads. Ericsson framed 6G as the first network generation designed as AI-native from specification stage.

May 2026Notable

Brazilian Academic Review Documents Domestic LLM Ecosystem: Amazônia IA, GAIA, Sabiá

UFSM's PET Information Systems group published a review of Brazil's homegrown LLM ecosystem on May 21, spotlighting Amazônia IA (WideLabs), GAIA (Google DeepMind/ABRIA/UFG), and Sabiá-2 (Maritaca AI) — all trained primarily on Brazilian Portuguese and national datasets including government documents, Brazilian literature, and academic articles. The review frames domestic LLMs as strategic assets for Brazilian AI sovereignty, particularly for legal and regulatory contexts that global models handle poorly. GAIA targets public-sector workflows, Sabiá focuses on scientific Portuguese, and Amazônia IA on broad enterprise use.

May 2026Landmark

OpenAI Reasoning Model Autonomously Disproves Erdős Unit-Distance Conjecture — An 80-Year Open Problem

OpenAI announced that an internal reasoning model independently disproved the planar unit-distance conjecture posed by Paul Erdős in 1946, producing an infinite family of examples demonstrating at least n^(1+δ) unit-distance pairs for a fixed positive constant δ — a polynomial improvement over the previous best. The proof introduces ideas from algebraic number theory into combinatorial geometry; Fields medalist Tim Gowers called it 'a milestone in AI mathematics.' It is the first time an AI autonomously solved a prominent open problem central to a mathematical subfield without human steering.

May 2026Notable

ISG: Brazilian Firms Scaling AI for ROI; Banking Closes Thousands of Branches via AI

ISG's 2026 Digital Business Innovation Services Brazil report found enterprises have shifted from AI experimentation to disciplined deployment targeting quantifiable ROI. In banking, AI adoption has driven the closure of thousands of physical branches as digital channels dominate. The report evaluated 48 providers and named Accenture, Globant, Stefanini, and Deloitte as Leaders across managed services, product engineering, and AI.
