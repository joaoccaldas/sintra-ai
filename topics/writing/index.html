May 2026Major

OpenAI Signs First Brazilian Media Deal with Folha de S.Paulo and UOL — Settles Scraping Lawsuit

OpenAI announced a landmark content licensing agreement with Grupo Folha (Folha de S.Paulo) and Grupo UOL, Brazil's two largest news organisations, integrating real-time journalism into ChatGPT responses with source attribution and links. The deal simultaneously settles a 2025 lawsuit Folha filed against OpenAI for unauthorised content scraping, and grants both groups access to ChatGPT Enterprise, Codex, and OpenAI APIs. Brazil ranks among the top three global ChatGPT markets with over 50 million monthly active users sending approximately 140 million daily messages.

May 2026Notable

Spotify Rolls Out AI-Powered Podcast Q&A for Premium Users in US, Sweden, and Ireland

Spotify launched an AI-powered question-and-answer feature for podcast listeners, enabling Premium mobile users to ask questions about episodes they are currently hearing, explore concepts mentioned by hosts, and receive topic-based recommendations. The feature launched first in the US, Sweden, and Ireland as part of a broader Investor Day announcement on May 21, 2026. A companion briefing-generation tool was also announced, letting users request audio summaries on specific topics.

May 2026Major

OpenAI and Google Partner on SynthID Watermarks and C2PA to Verify AI-Generated Images

OpenAI joined the C2PA Steering Committee on May 20, 2026 and partnered with Google DeepMind to embed SynthID invisible watermarks into all images generated through ChatGPT, Codex, and the OpenAI API. SynthID watermarks survive transformations like screenshots and format changes that strip standard metadata. OpenAI also launched a public verification tool at openai.com/verify where anyone can upload an image to check whether it originated from ChatGPT or the OpenAI API, with plans for cross-platform verification in coming months.

May 2026Notable

Amazon Alexa+ Generates On-Demand AI Podcasts from Any Topic

Amazon launched Alexa Podcasts on May 18, 2026, letting Alexa+ subscribers request a podcast on any subject and receive a fully structured audio episode narrated by two AI co-hosts. The feature taps licensing agreements with over 200 news organisations — including AP, Reuters, the Washington Post, Forbes, and Condé Nast — plus 200+ local U.S. newspapers. Alexa+ is included with Amazon Prime at no extra cost; non-Prime users pay $19.99/month.

May 2026Notable

Digital Platforms Warn of Technical Limits in Meeting Brazil's TSE AI Election Rules

Major digital platforms testified before Brazil's Chamber of Deputies in May 2026 that the TSE's 14 AI election resolutions present significant technical and legal compliance challenges. Companies warned that ambiguous terms like 'AI-manipulated material' could force over-removal of satire and legitimate AI-enhanced content to avoid fines and potential criminal liability. The hearing exposed a gap between the TSE's regulatory ambition and the real-world capabilities of automated content moderation at the scale of Brazilian social media — flagging implementation risks ahead of the October 2026 elections.

May 2026Notable

Digital Platforms Warn Brazil's TSE AI Election Rules Will Force Over-Removal of Legitimate Content

Major digital platforms testified before Brazil's Chamber of Deputies in May 2026 that the TSE's 14 AI election resolutions — including deepfake bans and mandatory AI labeling — present significant technical compliance challenges ahead of the October 2026 General Elections. Companies warned that ambiguous definitions of 'AI-manipulated material' could require aggressive over-removal of satire, commentary, and AI-enhanced content to avoid criminal liability. The hearing exposed a gap between regulatory ambition and automated moderation capabilities at Brazilian social-media scale.
