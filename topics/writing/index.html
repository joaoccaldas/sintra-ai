Jun 2026Notable

EU AI Office Closes Article 50 Transparency Consultation — Synthetic Content Labelling Rules Take Effect August 2

The European Commission's AI Office closed its public consultation on draft Article 50 transparency guidelines on June 3, ending a 26-day comment period. The 40-page draft mandates machine-readable labelling of synthetic audio, images, video, and text, and requires deployers to disclose emotion recognition and biometric categorisation. All obligations take effect August 2, 2026 — the Digital Omnibus did not defer Article 50, making these the AI Act's first enforcement-ready transparency rules.

Jun 2026Major

Microsoft Unveils Full MAI Model Family at Build 2026 — Seven Proprietary Models Reduce OpenAI Dependency

Microsoft AI CEO Mustafa Suleyman introduced seven MAI models at Build 2026 to reduce reliance on OpenAI APIs. The family includes MAI-Thinking-1 (sparse ~1T-parameter MoE reasoning model, trained entirely on commercially licensed data), MAI-Code-1-Flash (5B, integrating into VS Code and GitHub Copilot), MAI-Image-2.5, MAI-Transcribe-1.5, and MAI-Voice-2 (15 languages). All models are available through Azure AI Foundry and integrated across Microsoft 365 Copilot Wave 4.

Jun 2026Notable

Salesforce Signs Agreement to Acquire Contentful for ~$1.5B to Power Agentforce AI Content

Salesforce signed a definitive agreement to acquire Contentful, a composable content management platform trusted by over 4,800 enterprises. The deal gives Agentforce a native content layer capable of delivering AI-assembled, personalised digital experiences at scale. The transaction is expected to close in Salesforce's fiscal Q3 2027, pending regulatory approval.

May 2026Notable

Brazilian Healthtech Memed Raises R$80M ($14.1M) to Scale AI-Powered Digital Prescription Platform

São Paulo-based Memed raised R$80 million ($14.1 million) in a round co-led by DGF and BridgeOne, with participation from DNA Capital, to accelerate AI integration in its digital prescription infrastructure. Proceeds fund engineering, data-science hiring, and AI-driven clinical decision-support — drug interaction alerts, dosage recommendations — across Brazil's public and private healthcare systems. The round was the largest in Week 22 of the LatAm funding digest.

May 2026Major

OpenAI Signs First Brazilian Media Deal with Folha de S.Paulo and UOL — Settles Scraping Lawsuit

OpenAI announced a landmark content licensing agreement with Grupo Folha (Folha de S.Paulo) and Grupo UOL, Brazil's two largest news organisations, integrating real-time journalism into ChatGPT responses with source attribution and links. The deal simultaneously settles a 2025 lawsuit Folha filed against OpenAI for unauthorised content scraping, and grants both groups access to ChatGPT Enterprise, Codex, and OpenAI APIs. Brazil ranks among the top three global ChatGPT markets with over 50 million monthly active users sending approximately 140 million daily messages.

May 2026Notable

Spotify Rolls Out AI-Powered Podcast Q&A for Premium Users in US, Sweden, and Ireland

Spotify launched an AI-powered question-and-answer feature for podcast listeners, enabling Premium mobile users to ask questions about episodes they are currently hearing, explore concepts mentioned by hosts, and receive topic-based recommendations. The feature launched first in the US, Sweden, and Ireland as part of a broader Investor Day announcement on May 21, 2026. A companion briefing-generation tool was also announced, letting users request audio summaries on specific topics.
