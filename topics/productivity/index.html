Jul 2026Notable

Anthropic Launches 'Reflect,' a Usage-Habits Dashboard for Claude

Anthropic introduced Reflect on July 9, a beta dashboard on Free, Pro, and Max plans that shows a monthly recap of which topics users spent time on, their most active day and peak hour, plus optional break reminders and quiet hours. The feature requires memory to be enabled and ships on web and Claude Desktop first.

Jun 2026Notable

Meta Tests a Stand-Alone AI Companion App for Facebook Creators

Meta began testing a stand-alone AI companion app for Facebook creators — a reimagined Creator Studio with its recently launched AI creator assistant built in to help brainstorm content, analyze performance, and grow audiences. The app is positioned to retain creators against TikTok and YouTube and to reduce their reliance on third-party tools like ChatGPT.

Jun 2026Major

Anthropic Brings Scheduled Cron Jobs and Secure CLI Access to Managed Agents (Public Beta)

Anthropic announced public beta support for Claude Managed Agents that run on a cron schedule and securely call CLI tools and authenticated services without hosting custom infrastructure. Credentials are vault-stored and attached only at the network boundary to allowlisted domains, so secrets never enter the model's context — mitigating prompt-injection-based credential theft.

Jun 2026Notable

Alibaba's Accio Work Agentic AI Platform Expands to New Markets, Surpasses 10M Monthly Users

Alibaba International's Accio Work, a no-code agentic AI platform for SMEs, reported surpassing 10 million monthly active users as it expanded to new markets including Korea and Pakistan in late May/June 2026. The platform deploys autonomous agents for compliance (VAT filings, customs documentation across 100+ markets), supplier negotiation/RFQs, and logistics automation via WhatsApp and Telegram.

Jun 2026Notable

Make and Zapier Expand Autonomous 'AI Agent' Tiers Across No-Code Automation Platforms

No-code automation platforms Make and Zapier continued rolling out autonomous agent capabilities in 2026: Make's 'Maia' assistant builds automation scenarios from natural-language descriptions and now powers 'Make AI Agents' for autonomous task execution, while Zapier Agents read email, conduct research, and take actions across its 8,000+ app integrations and an MCP server exposing 30,000+ actions to external LLMs.

Jun 2026Major

Figure AI Confirms First Paid Commercial Deployment of Figure 03 Humanoid Robot at BMW Spartanburg — 40 Units

Figure AI confirmed commercial deployment of its Figure 03 humanoid robot at BMW's Spartanburg, South Carolina plant — the first paid commercial-scale deployment of a general-purpose humanoid robot at an industrial customer. The initial fleet of 40 Figure 03 units covers body-shop and assembly workstations including parts-handling, sub-assembly placement, and quality inspection at approximately $25/robot-hour. Expansion clauses cover additional Spartanburg workstations and BMW's German plants through 2027.
