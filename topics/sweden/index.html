Klarna CEO: Agentic Shopping Won't Erase Brand Loyalty, Pushes Open Agentic Commerce Protocol

Klarna CEO Sebastian Siemiatkowski argued that as AI shopping agents take over checkout, brand affinity will persist and matter more than UI design. Klarna has developed an open Agentic Commerce Protocol allowing merchants to catalog products for discovery by AI agents, positioned as complementary to the Stripe/OpenAI Agentic Commerce Protocol.

Jun 2026Notable

Ericsson Reveals AI-Native Engineering Strategy at Snowflake Summit: 1,500 Engineers, Weeks-to-Hours Compression

Elena Fersman, VP and Head of AI Innovation at Ericsson, presented the company's AI transformation thesis at Snowflake Summit 2026 in San Francisco: that unified data access must precede meaningful AI deployment. Ericsson has deployed a coordinated stack of AI personas, agents, and skills to 1,500+ engineers, compressing specification work from weeks to hours. The company is partnering with Snowflake to provide the data platform backbone for its 'AI-ification' strategy across its 150-year-old technology portfolio.

Jun 2026Notable

ServiceNow Hosts Enterprise AI Summit in Stockholm — Swedish Enterprise AI Adoption Reaches 35%

ServiceNow held its AI Summit Stockholm at Quality Hotel Strawberry Arena on June 4, one stop in a global series targeting enterprise AI adoption. The event focused on AI agents, enterprise automation, customer service workflows, and IT operations transformation. Swedish enterprise AI adoption has risen to 35% among firms with 10+ employees, up from 25% in 2024 — a 40% year-over-year increase that marks Stockholm as a top-tier enterprise AI market for global SaaS providers.

Jun 2026Major

Mistral AI Breaks Ground on €1.2B Swedish AI Datacenter with EcoDataCenter in Borlänge

Mistral AI and EcoDataCenter formally broke ground on a €1.2 billion AI datacenter facility in Borlänge, Sweden — Mistral's first AI infrastructure investment outside France. The facility will host NVIDIA Vera Rubin GPUs and is scheduled to open in 2027, making Sweden one of Europe's largest recipients of dedicated AI infrastructure investment from a European AI lab.

Jun 2026Major

Sweden's Arrhenius AI Supercomputer Enters Full Production at NAISS

The Arrhenius AI supercomputer, operated by the National Academic Infrastructure for Supercomputing in Sweden (NAISS) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, entered full production capacity with 4,096 NVIDIA H100 GPUs delivering 16 exaFLOPS of AI compute. The system is the largest academic AI supercomputer in the Nordic region and is allocated via the Swedish Research Council to universities and research institutions across Sweden.

Jun 2026Major

Microsoft Completes $3 B AI Datacenter Expansion in Sweden, Bringing Total to 200k+ Servers

Microsoft announced the completion of its $3 billion AI datacenter expansion programme in Sweden, adding two new halls at the Gävle campus running entirely on renewable hydropower. The expansion brings Microsoft's total Sweden server count to over 200,000, making it the hyperscaler's largest European AI datacenter footprint. The facility will serve Azure AI customers across EMEA with data residency in the EU.
