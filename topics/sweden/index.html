Jun 2026Major

Mistral AI Breaks Ground on €1.2B Swedish AI Datacenter with EcoDataCenter in Borlänge

Mistral AI and EcoDataCenter formally broke ground on a €1.2 billion AI datacenter facility in Borlänge, Sweden — Mistral's first AI infrastructure investment outside France. The facility will host NVIDIA Vera Rubin GPUs and is scheduled to open in 2027, making Sweden one of Europe's largest recipients of dedicated AI infrastructure investment from a European AI lab.

Jun 2026Major

Sweden's Arrhenius AI Supercomputer Enters Full Production at NAISS

The Arrhenius AI supercomputer, operated by the National Academic Infrastructure for Supercomputing in Sweden (NAISS) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, entered full production capacity with 4,096 NVIDIA H100 GPUs delivering 16 exaFLOPS of AI compute. The system is the largest academic AI supercomputer in the Nordic region and is allocated via the Swedish Research Council to universities and research institutions across Sweden.

Jun 2026Major

Microsoft Completes $3 B AI Datacenter Expansion in Sweden, Bringing Total to 200k+ Servers

Microsoft announced the completion of its $3 billion AI datacenter expansion programme in Sweden, adding two new halls at the Gävle campus running entirely on renewable hydropower. The expansion brings Microsoft's total Sweden server count to over 200,000, making it the hyperscaler's largest European AI datacenter footprint. The facility will serve Azure AI customers across EMEA with data residency in the EU.

May 2026Notable

Eurostat: Denmark 42%, Finland 37.8%, Sweden 35% Lead EU Enterprise AI Adoption — EU Avg 20%

Eurostat's 2025 ICT enterprise survey, widely reported in May 2026, confirms Nordic countries dominate European AI adoption. Denmark leads at 42.0%, Finland at 37.8%, Sweden at 35.0% (joint 3rd with Belgium). The EU27 average is 20.0% — meaning Nordic enterprises are nearly twice as likely to use AI as the continental average. Large enterprises (250+ employees) continue to lead, but SME adoption is accelerating fastest in the Nordics. Sweden recorded the highest 2-year growth of any EU nation, up approximately 14.7 percentage points since 2023.

May 2026Notable

Nordic Nations Launch NNAI Consortium to Build Shared LLM for Scandinavian Languages

The governments of Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and Finland jointly launched the Nordic National AI (NNAI) Consortium with €120 million in public funding to develop a shared large language model optimised for Scandinavian languages. The consortium will train a 70-billion-parameter model on national library corpora, public records, and government datasets with a target release in 2027. The project is hosted by the Nordic Council of Ministers.

May 2026Major

Ericsson Leaves Kista for Hagastaden — Sweden's Largest-Ever Office Lease, 71,000 sq m over 15 Years

Ericsson announced it will relocate its Stockholm headquarters from Kista — once dubbed Sweden's Silicon Valley — to the Hagastaden district, signing 15-year leases for approximately 71,000 sq m across five properties with Atrium Ljungberg and Castellum, the largest single-tenant office lease ever completed in Sweden. The urban campus, adjacent to Karolinska University Hospital, KI, and new residential areas, is designed to attract AI and R&D talent. The physical move is expected to begin in early 2028.
