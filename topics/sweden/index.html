Jun 2026Notable

Ericsson and Telia Launch 'Digital Arena Sweden' National 5G/6G/AI Test Center

Ericsson, Telia, RISE (and subsidiary AstaZero), Future by Lund, Lund University and KTH launched Digital Arena Sweden, a national pre-commercial 5G, 6G and AI test center announced at the Almedalen forum on Gotland. The initiative represents an investment of over SEK 300 million (about $31 million), supported by Vinnova through the Avancerad Digitalisering program, and targets industries from mining and transport to defense and medtech.

Jun 2026Notable

CoreWeave Partners With Conapto to Expand Renewable-Powered AI Cloud in Sweden

AI hyperscaler CoreWeave signed a colocation agreement with Stockholm-based Conapto spanning two campuses, with initial capacity already online at Stockholm 4 South, powered by 100% renewable energy with heat recovery into Stockholm's district heating network. The deal brings CoreWeave's European sites to eight; as of March 31, 2026 CoreWeave operated 49 data centers globally with over 1GW active and 3.5GW+ contracted power.

Jun 2026Notable

Stockholm's Fika Jobs Raises $4M for AI-Agent Video Hiring Platform

Stockholm-based Fika Jobs raised a $4 million pre-seed round led by Luminar Ventures, with Alliance VC and Candy Crush / King co-founders Sebastian Knutsson and Riccardo Zacconi participating. The platform pairs short-form video profiles with AI interview agents (currently powered by Google's Gemini) that generate personalized questions and run roughly 10-minute candidate interviews. It is free for job seekers; employers pay 10% of a successful hire's first-year salary, with about 100 companies on the waitlist.

Jun 2026Notable

Sweden's AI-Native Startups More Than Triple Funding to €454M in 2026

Sweden's AI-native startups have more than tripled their funding so far in 2026, reaching roughly €454 million, led by names like app-builder Lovable and legaltech Legora. The surge underscores Stockholm's position as one of Europe's densest AI startup hubs, even as Swedish founders warn that the national AI strategy isn't enough to stop talent drain to San Francisco.

Jun 2026Major

IMF Warning Highlights Sweden's Exposure to AI-Driven Cyber Risk

The IMF and Swedish cybersecurity specialists warned that AI can shorten the time needed to discover and exploit software vulnerabilities. Sweden's extensive digitalization and dependence on shared services such as BankID and Swish were identified as factors that could amplify coordinated disruption.

Jun 2026Notable

Sweden's Legora and Ironclad Launch Bidirectional 'AI-to-AI' Legal Integration

Stockholm-founded legal-AI platform Legora and US contract-lifecycle AI platform Ironclad announced a bidirectional integration connecting Legora's legal research and analysis AI with Ironclad's contracting AI. Legora CEO Max Junestrand said it is the first time two legal AI platforms have been connected this deeply and in both directions; rollout is phased to mutual customers.
