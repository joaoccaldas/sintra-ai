May 2026Major

Ericsson Leaves Kista for Hagastaden — Sweden's Largest-Ever Office Lease, 71,000 sq m over 15 Years

Ericsson announced it will relocate its Stockholm headquarters from Kista — once dubbed Sweden's Silicon Valley — to the Hagastaden district, signing 15-year leases for approximately 71,000 sq m across five properties with Atrium Ljungberg and Castellum, the largest single-tenant office lease ever completed in Sweden. The urban campus, adjacent to Karolinska University Hospital, KI, and new residential areas, is designed to attract AI and R&D talent. The physical move is expected to begin in early 2028.

May 2026Major

Sweden and US Sign Technology Prosperity Deal — AI, 5G/6G, Quantum, and Defence Innovation

Swedish Foreign Minister Maria Malmer Stenergard and US Secretary of State Marco Rubio signed a bilateral Technology Prosperity Deal in Helsingborg, committing both governments to deepened cooperation across artificial intelligence, 5G/6G, quantum computing, biomedical research, advanced manufacturing, space, and defence innovation. The MOU follows similar frameworks signed with the UK, Japan, and South Korea. Ericsson immediately welcomed the deal, citing its implications for 6G AI research collaboration between the two countries.

May 2026Major

SCB: 35% of Swedish Enterprises Used AI in 2025 — Up 10pp YoY; 37% of Adults Use Generative AI

Statistics Sweden (SCB) published 'Artificial Intelligence in Sweden 2026', finding that 35% of Swedish enterprises used AI in 2025, a 10 percentage point increase from 2024, with large enterprises (250+ employees) leading at 71.9% adoption. Among the general population aged 16+, generative AI use climbed from 25% to 37% year-on-year. Sweden ranked in the EU top five for both in-house AI development and combined AI, cloud, and data analytics adoption.

May 2026Major

Ericsson Deploys SAP Business Data Fabric and Joule AI to 85,000 Users

At SAP Sapphire 2026 in Madrid (May 19–21), Ericsson announced that over 85,000 employees are live on SAP Business Data Cloud with the Joule AI copilot, moving from AI experimentation to enterprise-wide execution via a federated data architecture. Ericsson consolidated its operational data across ERP, HR, and finance systems so Joule can answer cross-domain queries with consistent context. It is among the largest live enterprise AI rollouts on the SAP platform globally.

May 2026Notable

Spotify Launches ElevenLabs-Powered AI Audiobook Narration for Self-Published Authors

At Investor Day 2026, Spotify unveiled an AI audiobook creation tool for its Spotify for Authors platform, powered by ElevenLabs voice synthesis, allowing authors to generate professional narrations without a recording studio. The tool launches in invite-only beta in June 2026, initially supporting English with expansion to 10 languages including Swedish planned for late 2026. Spotify hosts over 700,000 audiobook titles and sees this as a key path to growing its long-tail catalog without recording costs.

May 2026Major

Vinnova's Sweden–Canada Programme Brings Clinical AI to Karolinska, Sahlgrenska, and Skåne Hospitals

Vinnova published results of its Sweden–Canada AI healthcare collaboration, bringing Karolinska University Hospital, Sahlgrenska University Hospital, and Skåne University Hospital together with Unity Health Toronto, coordinated by AI Sweden. The initiative focused on a concrete clinical challenge — managing missed patient appointments — and demonstrated how AI can move from pilot to daily clinical practice. The project is cited by both governments as a model for responsible AI maturity in public healthcare.
