Salesforce Summer '26: Multi-Agent Orchestration and Agentforce Operations Generally Available

Salesforce's Summer 2026 release delivers Multi-Agent Orchestration enabling agents to collaborate as a unified team with shared context across all CRM channels, an Agentforce Self-Service Portal with AI-native UI, and Agentforce Operations targeting end-to-end back-office automation (AP, AR, procurement, HR onboarding). Tableau MCP integration allows AI agents to query analytics directly. Features are generally available from June 2–15, 2026.

Jun 2026Notable

Snowflake and Anthropic: Claude Runs Inside Cortex AI — No Data Egress, Full Governance

At Snowflake Summit 26 in San Francisco, Snowflake and Anthropic detailed the technical architecture of their $200M partnership: Claude models run inside Snowflake Cortex AI natively, processing customer data without egress, fully governed by Snowflake's row- and column-level security policies. Enterprises can invoke Claude via SQL or Python directly in Snowflake Notebooks. The integration addresses the #1 blocker to enterprise AI adoption in financial services, healthcare, and insurance.

Jun 2026Notable

Salesforce Signs Agreement to Acquire Contentful for ~$1.5B to Power Agentforce AI Content

Salesforce signed a definitive agreement to acquire Contentful, a composable content management platform trusted by over 4,800 enterprises. The deal gives Agentforce a native content layer capable of delivering AI-assembled, personalised digital experiences at scale. The transaction is expected to close in Salesforce's fiscal Q3 2027, pending regulatory approval.

May 2026Notable

OpenAI Publishes Frontier Governance Framework Aligned with EU AI Act

OpenAI released its Frontier Governance Framework on May 28, aligning safety practices with California's Transparency in Frontier AI Act and the EU AI Act's Code of Practice for General Purpose AI. The document covers risk assessment across cyber offense, CBRN threats, harmful manipulation, and loss-of-control risks, and supersedes the earlier Preparedness Framework. It includes protocols for model reporting, security incident response, and external expert oversight.

May 2026Major

EU AI Act Full Applicability Countdown: 66 Days to August 2 — Compliance Status Across Industries

With August 2, 2026 approaching — the date the EU AI Act becomes fully applicable to all AI systems except high-risk Annex III (deferred to December 2027) — compliance consultancies are documenting significant readiness gaps. Financial services and healthcare sectors are furthest along; retail, HR tech, and marketing AI lag. The EU AI Office has 27 national competent authorities in final setup phases. Companies failing to classify AI systems, document conformity assessments, or register GPAI models face fines up to €35M or 7% of global turnover from day one of enforcement.

May 2026Notable

Asana Acquires AI Agent Builder StackAI for $75M to Build Human-Agent OS

Asana acquired no-code AI workflow automation startup StackAI for $75 million to add cross-system agent execution to its work management platform. StackAI enables agents to take actions inside Salesforce, Slack, Google Workspace, and other enterprise systems without code. Asana positioned the acquisition as building toward an 'operating system for human-agent teams'.
