Until it doesn't.

Adoption & exponentials

AI adoption is fast — and the curves are steep

Fast adoption

ChatGPT reached 100 million users in just two months — faster than the telephone (75 years) or the internet (7 years). Adoption is now the slow part.

Exponentials

Frontier models surpass human experts while the cost of intelligence collapses. Training compute doubles every six months. Linear intuition fails on curves that double.
