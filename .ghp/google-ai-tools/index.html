Google AI Ecosystem

Every Google AI Tool
Explained

A curated map of the most powerful AI tools from Google, from core research assistants to experimental world-generators.

←Back to Sintra AI

Core & Research

Gemini

Google's primary AI chatbot for questions, image generation, and file creation.

Multi-modal interaction and Google Ecosystem integration.

Notebook LM

AI-powered research assistant that organizes sources into notebooks.

Creates infographics, slide decks, and audio overviews from sources.

Illuminate

Advanced audio dialogue generator based on provided sources.

Deep customization of host/guest voices and conversation style.

Creativity & Media

Google Vids

Online video editing tool with AI generation capabilities.

AI voiceovers and automated storyboard-to-video conversion.

Google Flow (Images/Video)

Professional-grade generative tool for precise visual control.

Aspect ratio control, model selection (Nano Banana Pro), and annotation editing.

Google Flow Music

AI-driven music production tool with an interactive voice mode.

Real-time song collaboration through natural language.

Music Effects DJ

Interactive music exploration and remixing tool.

Prompt-based instrument adding and BPM control.

Business & Productivity

Pamelli

Business DNA extractor for marketing assets.

Analyzes website to generate brand colors, fonts, and social media posts.

Workspace Studio

Automation engine for Google Workspace (Zapier-like).

Trigger-based flows for Gmail, Chat, and Form responses.

CC

Personal productivity agent for morning reports.

Automatic synthesis of Gmail, Calendar, and Drive updates.

Learn Your Way

Personalized learning platform for textbooks.

Transforms static text into dynamic quizzes, slides, and mind maps.

Experimental & Beta

Project Genie

Generative immersive virtual world engine.

Text-to-world generation with real-time navigation.

Disco

AI-native browser that turns tabs into interactive apps.

Consolidates open-tab information into a custom mini-app.

Mixboard

Interactive canvas for creative mood-boarding.

Blends text and image prompts to refine visual concepts.

Developer & Coding

Google AI Studio

Prototyping playground for Gemini models.

Direct access to Nano Banana Pro, Imagen, and specialized models.

Opal

User-friendly "Vibe Coding" app builder.

Creates functional apps directly within the Gemini ecosystem.

Anti-gravity

Local AI agent for macOS file interaction.

Direct local file updates and third-party integration.

Firebase Studio

Fullstack AI development environment.

Unified backend and frontend build for mobile apps.

Jewels

Autonomous agent coding system.

End-to-end autonomous code generation and implementation.

Integrated Features

AI Search & Chrome

AI mode in Google Search and "Ask Gemini" browser sidebar.

Auto-browse navigation and repeatable prompt "Skills".

Gmail AI

AI Inbox and "Help me write" assistant.

Automatic categorization and draft polishing.

Drive & Docs

Project-based AI chat and Gemini integration in Docs/Sheets.

Canvas for interactive dashboards and automated formula building.

Google Meet

AI-powered meeting notes and real-time translation.

Automated transcription and cross-language communication.

The Google AI landscape is evolving rapidly. Most of these tools are available via Google Workspace or experimental lab accounts.
